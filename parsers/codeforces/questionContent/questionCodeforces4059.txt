A. Vasya and Chocolate
time limit per test1 second
memory limit per test256 megabytes
inputstandard input
outputstandard output

a

b

Vasya currently has

1≤t≤100

10
9
)

Output
Print
t

i

i
-th test.

Copy
2
10 3 1 1
1000000000 1 1000000000 1

13
1000000001000000000
